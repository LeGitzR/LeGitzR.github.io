Water Recycling We Offer

Sales and installation of water recycling plants for both car wash and irrigation systems. Use of a water recycling plant can reduce water usage on a car wash machine by up to 80% and when coupled with a rain catch system can save up to 100% of water during rainy seasons. We also offer similar systems which can be used for irrigation systems as well as household water saving.
